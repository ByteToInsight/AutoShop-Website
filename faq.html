Shipping & Logistics

What is the delivery timeline for Pan-India shipping?

expand_more

We offer comprehensive shipping across India. Metro cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, etc.) typically receive orders within 3-5 business days. Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities may take 5-9 business days depending on local courier logistics. Shipping is free for orders above ₹25,000.

How do I track my high-value automotive components?

expand_more

All high-performance parts are shipped via insured premium carriers. You will receive a real-time tracking link once the technical audit is complete. For engine assemblies or suspension kits, we use specialized surface transport with extra palletizing to ensure zero damage.

Installation & Partners

Are there certified installation partners in my city?

expand_more

Yes, we have established a network of premier performance workshops in Tier 1 and Tier 2 cities across India. From Bengaluru to Chandigarh, our partners are trained in AUTOSHOP standards. Installation charges typically range from ₹2,000 to ₹15,000 depending on complexity.

Does local installation affect my warranty?

expand_more

To maintain the full manufacturer warranty, we recommend installation through our "Partner Locator" shops. Installation by non-certified local mechanics on sensitive ECU or internal engine components may void technical support and return eligibility.

Billing & GST Compliance

Can I get a GST invoice for my business?

expand_more

Absolutely. During checkout, ensure you select the "B2B / GST Invoice" option and provide your valid GSTIN and company name. We issue a tax-compliant invoice so you can claim Input Tax Credit (ITC) for your automotive services or business vehicle maintenance.

Are the prices inclusive of all taxes?

expand_more

All prices displayed on AUTOSHOP.in are inclusive of GST. There are no hidden charges. Customs duties on imported performance components are pre-calculated and included in the final ₹ price shown at the summary page.
